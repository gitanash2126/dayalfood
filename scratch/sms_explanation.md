# Why Free SMS is Impossible

1. **Firebase**: Requires a Debit/Credit card linked to Google Cloud (`auth/billing-not-enabled`).
2. **Fast2SMS / Msg91**: Requires DLT (Business) Registration as per TRAI guidelines to send OTPs.
3. **Twilio**: Trial accounts only send SMS to numbers you manually verify in their dashboard.

## Workarounds
1. Attach a card to Google Cloud (You get 10k free verifications/month, but card is mandatory).
2. Switch from Mobile OTP to **Email OTP** (100% Free via Gmail).
